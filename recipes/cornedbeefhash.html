Puerto Rican Cornbeef Hash

Description

Corned beef hash is a flavorful dish of finely chopped cooked corned beef hash(canned corn beef),diced potatoes( or fried french fries) and different spices. Usually prepared alongside white rice and platanos maduros.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of sofrito
  • 1 teabspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 packet of sazon
  • 8 ounces of tomato sauce
  • 2 cans of corned beef
  • 2 tablespoons of water
  • 1/4-1/2 cup of corn kernels

Instructions

  1. Heat oil over medium high heat in a large pan.
  2. Add in sofrito, spices, and tomato sauce. Cook for a minute before adding in corned beef.
  3. Break down the corned beef with a spoon and allow to cook for two minutes before adding in the remaining ingredients. Mix and cook for three minutes while mixing occasionally before lowering the heat to a medium and closing with a lid.
  4. Cook for 15 minutes or until potaties are fork tender.
  5. Taste and season with salt, if desired.
  6. Serve with white rice.
